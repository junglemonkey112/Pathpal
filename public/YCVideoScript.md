# PathPal - One-Minute Video Script
## YC Application Video

---

## Video Specs
- **Duration**: 60 seconds
- **Tone**: Sincere, enthusiastic, confident
- **Setting**: Simple background (white wall or desk), face the camera

---

## Full Script

### [0:00-0:05] Opening (Hook)

> "I almost paid $15,000 for a college admission consultant. Then I realized there was a better way."

---

### [0:05-0:15] The Problem

> "Every year, millions of students apply to US colleges. The competition is fierce—Harvard accepts just 3.4% of applicants. Parents are desperate for help, and they're willing to pay anything."

---

### [0:15-0:25] The Issue

> "But here's the problem: good consultants charge $200-500 per hour. Most families can't afford that. And there's no way to know if you're getting real value."

---

### [0:25-0:40] The Solution

> "That's why I built PathPal. We're a marketplace connecting students with affordable college consultants—current college students who charge $30-80 per hour but have been through the process themselves."

> "We use AI to match students with the right consultant, and we have a community forum where students share real experiences. No sales pressure, just honest advice."

---

### [0:40-0:50] Progress + Vision

> "We've launched our MVP and already have [X] consultants and [X] students. We believe every student deserves access to good guidance, regardless of their background."

---

### [0:50-1:00] Closing

> "I'm [name], and I'm applying to YC to scale PathPal and help millions of students make their college dreams a reality. Thank you."

---

## Filming Tips

### Camera Setup
- **Position**: Camera at eye level
- **Distance**: Face fills about 2/3 of frame
- **Lighting**: Natural light from window (soft, not direct)
- **Background**: Clean, uncluttered (white wall works great)

### Delivery
- Speak naturally, not rushed
- Make eye contact with camera
- Smile at the beginning and end
- Practice 3-5 times before recording

### Equipment
- **Best**: Smartphone on tripod
- **OK**: Laptop webcam
- **Audio**: Quiet room, no background noise

### Editing (if needed)
- Keep it simple - no fancy effects
- Add your name as subtitle
- End with a clear call to action
