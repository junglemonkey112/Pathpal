## Section 3: Idea & Insight

**18. Why this idea?**
> I went through the college application process myself and saw how broken the consulting market was. Parents were paying $10k+ for opaque services with no transparency. I realized that current college students could provide valuable guidance at a fraction of the cost—they've been through the process recently and understand what works today. The market is huge ($3B+ in the US alone), and no one is serving this segment well.

**19. What's new?**
> 1) Student consultants (vs. professional consultants) at 1/3 the price
> 2) AI-powered matching to reduce search costs
> 3) Community forum to build trust and content moat

**20. What do you understand that others don't?**
> The "peer advantage" in college consulting is undervalued. Current students who got into top schools recently know exactly what admissions officers want today. They're more relatable and affordable than veteran consultants who may be out of touch.

**21. Competitors:**
| Competitor | Weakness |
|---|---|
| The Princeton Review | Expensive, sales-focused |
| Ivy League Consulting | Premium pricing, opaque |
| Reddit/r/ApplyingToCollege | Unstructured, unverified advice |

**Our advantage:** Structured marketplace + AI matching + verified reviews + transparent pricing

# 💡 额外建议

## 让你的申请脱颖欲出的关键

### 1. **Founder 故事要真实动人**
> 不要只说 "I saw a problem"，要说 "I experienced this problem myself"

### 2. **数据说话**
> 即使是 mock 数据，也要看起来合理：
> - Waitlist: 200+ students
> - Conversion: 15% of visitors register

### 3. **Video 技巧**
> - 自然光线，面对镜头
> - 讲一个真实的用户故事
> - 控制在 60 秒
> - 结尾清晰有力

### 4. **回答要简洁**
> YC 每份申请只有 10-15 分钟审核时间
> 用 bullet points，不要废话

### 5. **展现 founder market fit**
> 如果你有教育/留学背景，一定要强调
> 如果你是技术背景，强调你可以 build the product
